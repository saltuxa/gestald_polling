Gestald Polling Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Information We Use

When a viewer shares Twitch identity with the extension, Gestald Polling may use the viewer's Twitch user ID and display name. Poll votes, selected answer options, channel ID, and timestamps may also be processed while a poll is active.

Why We Use It

This information is used to allow voting, prevent duplicate votes, show live poll results, and display the list of voters according to the privacy mode selected by the broadcaster or moderator.

Poll Privacy Modes

In anonymous choice mode, viewers may be shown as having voted, but their selected answer is hidden. In public choice mode, the selected answer may be shown next to the viewer's display name.

Storage

The current MVP stores active poll data in server memory only. Poll data is cleared when the server restarts or when the in-memory state is reset. No long-term database storage is used in the current version.

Sharing

Gestald Polling does not sell personal data. Poll information is shown only as part of the Twitch Extension experience for the relevant channel.

Your Choices

Voting requires Twitch identity sharing. Viewers who do not share identity may be unable to vote. You can manage Twitch Extension permissions through Twitch account and extension settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the extension owner through the Twitch developer account that publishes Gestald Polling.
