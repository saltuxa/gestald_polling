Gestald Polling Terms of Service

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Use of the Extension

Gestald Polling lets Twitch streamers and moderators create live polls and lets viewers vote in those polls through the Twitch Extension interface.

Voting

Each linked Twitch user may cast one vote per active poll. Votes cannot be changed after submission in the current version.

Streamer Control

Broadcasters and authorized moderators are responsible for poll questions, answer options, and how poll results are used during a stream.

Availability

The extension is provided as-is. Polls may be unavailable during maintenance, hosting outages, Twitch platform issues, or extension updates.

Data

The extension uses Twitch identity information only to support voting, prevent duplicate votes, and show voter lists according to the selected poll privacy mode. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Contact

For questions about this extension, contact the extension owner through the Twitch developer account that publishes Gestald Polling.
